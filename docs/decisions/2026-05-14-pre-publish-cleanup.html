Recommendation

★ Pick B — Minimum + token trim

It's the only option that fixes the broken pointers and earns a real token saving with no UX regression. The four demoted skills already say "use when X" in their descriptions — they were never auto-firing for general work. Tier2 just makes that explicit and stops charging context for skills nobody is reading.

Flip to A if you want to ship today and trust users to opt-in to niche skills via aikit add. Flip to C if you've changed your mind on the "always available" ask for /directions and /roadmap after seeing they don't auto-fire.
